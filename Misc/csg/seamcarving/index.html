The Good

Comment:

The results are not fantanstic, but are tolerable. The added seams are visible, but overall since the background is not so fancy, its just plain for most part, so the results we got are okay when compared with the results the program yielded for other images.

The Bad

Comment:

The results are not so good. But still the house stayed fine to some extent! Some portion of the image are stretched so much that it formed the weird pattern of repetition.

The ugly

Comment:

As expected the program had trouble with images consisting something like face. The outputs are ugly they didn't turn out be well, some part of the face are stretched and shrinked so much that they don't resemble the reality.
